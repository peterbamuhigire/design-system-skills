# MANIFEST - fonts/08-body-ui-workhorses

Quiet, legible body and UI faces used under a more distinctive display face. These are the calm
layer, not the whole identity. Binaries are gitignored; drop files in per device.

| Family | Role | Voice | Best for | Avoid for | Pair with | Licence | Embed? | Redistribute file? | Present? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Public Sans | Body/UI | Civic, neutral, reliable | Reports, public-service UI, forms, long body copy | Standalone identity/display | Fraunces, Source Serif 4, Bricolage Grotesque | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |
| Hanken Grotesk | Body/UI | Soft, modern, polished | SaaS body, decks, landing-page sections, mobile UI | Monotype-only artifacts | Bricolage Grotesque, Clash Display, Fraunces | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |
| Source Sans 3 | Body only | Quiet, utilitarian, readable | Body under strong display faces | Primary/display/standalone use | Fraunces, Source Serif 4, Bricolage Grotesque | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in (body only) |
| IBM Plex Sans | UI/body | Technical-neutral, rational | Technical UI body, docs, dashboards | Expressive headlines alone | IBM Plex Serif, IBM Plex Mono, Unbounded | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |
| Atkinson Hyperlegible | Body/UI | Accessible, legibility-first | Forms, healthcare, education, accessibility-sensitive UI | Luxury/display identity | Fraunces, Newsreader, Public Sans | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |

**Hard exclusion:** Geist is banned and must not appear here. Source Sans 3 is permitted only as
a paired body face, never as display or standalone primary type.
