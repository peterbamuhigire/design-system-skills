# MANIFEST - fonts/05-friendly-humanist

Warm, accessible, service-oriented type for healthcare, education, NGO/public-service products,
onboarding, support flows, and human-centred interfaces. Friendly does not mean childish.
Binaries are gitignored; drop files in per device.

| Family | Role | Voice | Best for | Avoid for | Pair with | Licence | Embed? | Redistribute file? | Present? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Atkinson Hyperlegible | UI/body | Accessible, humane, clear | Healthcare, education, forms, older-user interfaces | Luxury/editorial display | Fraunces, Newsreader, Public Sans | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |
| Lexend | UI/body | Calm, readable, learning-friendly | Education, onboarding, service apps | Formal statutory reports | Bricolage Grotesque, Fraunces | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |
| Alegreya Sans | Body/UI | Warm, literary-humanist | Education, culture, social-impact documents | Dense technical dashboards | Alegreya, Fraunces | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |
| Hanken Grotesk | Body/UI | Soft, polished, modern | Friendly SaaS body, mobile UI, deck body | As the only typeface in an artifact | Bricolage Grotesque, Clash Display, Fraunces | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |
| Public Sans | Body/UI | Civic, plainspoken, accessible | Public-sector UI, forms, service pages | Expressive headlines by itself | Source Serif 4, Fraunces, Bricolage Grotesque | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |

**House rule:** avoid banned friendly cliches such as Nunito/Nunito Sans. Pick this category when
the brief says warm, accessible, civic, healthcare, education, NGO, service, onboarding, or
plainspoken.
