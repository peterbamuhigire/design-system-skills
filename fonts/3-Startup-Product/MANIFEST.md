# MANIFEST — fonts/3-Startup-Product

Families for **startup & product** artifacts (landing pages, SaaS marketing, pitch decks).
Binaries gitignored; drop files in per device.

| Family | Styles expected | Format | Licence | Embed? | Redistribute file? | Present? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Bricolage Grotesque | ExtraLight–ExtraBold | TTF/woff2 | OFL | Yes | Allowed (kept out by policy) | drop-in |
| Clash Display | Variable / multiple weights | OTF/woff2 | **Fontshare** | **Yes** | **NO** | manual download from fontshare.com |
| Satoshi | Variable / multiple weights | OTF/woff2 | **Fontshare** | **Yes** | **NO** | manual download from fontshare.com |
| Cabinet Grotesk | Variable / multiple weights | OTF/woff2 | **Fontshare** | **Yes** | **NO** | manual download from fontshare.com |
| General Sans | Variable / multiple weights | OTF/woff2 | **Fontshare** | **Yes** | **NO** | manual download from fontshare.com |

**Critical licence note:** the four Fontshare families are free to **use and embed** in built
artifacts (web/app/doc) but their **files may not be redistributed**. Never commit or ship the
raw `.otf`/`.woff2` for these — embed into the output only. Download manually from
https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/ on each device. Bricolage Grotesque (OFL) is the always-
available baseline anchor when the Fontshare faces are absent.
